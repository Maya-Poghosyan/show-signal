SECTION 1: NON-EMERGENCY MEDICAL TRANSPORTATION (NEMT)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

TENNCARE / TENNESSEE CARRIERS (State Program)
  - Free non-emergency medical transportation for TennCare (Medicaid) members
  - Must schedule rides at least 2 business days in advance
  - Pickup at home address on file (one exception allowed per year)
  - Round-trip bus tickets available for those near a bus stop
  - Mileage Reimbursement Program available for eligible households
    with their own transportation
  - Schedule at: Tennessee Carriers member portal
  - Note: New rules effective 7/1/2025 — pickup only at address on file

PRIMECARE TRANSPORT
  - Ambulatory, wheelchair, and stretcher transport
  - Serves Nashville and Clarksville areas
  - Medical and leisure trips for patients, seniors, and disabled individuals
  - Website: primecare-nashville.com

ADI MEDICAL TRANSPORTATION SERVICE
  - Specializes in transport for individuals with disabilities
  - Covers doctor, dentist, dialysis, and leisure trips
  - Serves Nashville and surrounding communities
  - Website: aditransportation.com

HEALING HEARTS TRANSPORTATION
  - Non-emergency medical transport in the Nashville area
  - Listed among top-rated local NEMT providers (Yelp)

HELPING HANDS NEMT TRANSPORTS
  - Non-emergency medical transport in the Nashville area
  - Listed among top-rated local NEMT providers (Yelp)

UBER HEALTH (Healthcare Facility Program)
  - Non-emergency medical transportation coordinated by care providers
  - Available for ambulatory patients and wheelchair users
  - Rides schedulable on demand or up to 30 days in advance
  - Notifications sent to patients in 15+ languages
  - Note: Must be arranged through a healthcare coordinator/dashboard,
    not directly by patients via the standard app
  - Website: uberhealth.com

SECTION 2: LONG-DISTANCE & AIR MEDICAL TRANSPORT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

EASTERN ROYAL MEDICAL TRANSPORT (ERMT)
  - Long-distance, bedside-to-bedside medical transport
  - Licensed medical personnel accompany every transport
  - Provides oxygen support, catheter care, and medication assistance
  - Coordinates with Vanderbilt, Methodist, UT Medical Center, and Erlanger
  - Phone: 800-696-1495
  - Website: easternroyalmedical.com

AIR AMBULANCE WORLDWIDE
  - Air ambulance, commercial stretchers, and medical escort services
  - Bedside-to-bedside assistance with 2+ medical personnel
  - Works with Nashville-area hospitals; handles all logistics
  - Three service levels: air ambulance, commercial stretcher, medical escort
  - Website: airambulanceworldwide.com

SECTION 3: LANGUAGE INTERPRETATION & TRANSLATION SERVICES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

METRO PUBLIC HEALTH DEPARTMENT — INTERPRETER SERVICES (FREE)
  - Free interpreter services for all health department visits
  - Available onsite or by phone upon request
  - Contact: 615-340-8963

VANDERBILT UNIVERSITY MEDICAL CENTER (VUMC) — INTERPRETER SERVICES (FREE)
  - Free interpreter services for all patients (required by federal law)
  - Spanish interpreter phone line: 844-969-2258
  - Deaf/hard-of-hearing patients: dial 711 (TTY/Tennessee Relay Service)
  - Braille documents and reader assistance: 615-322-7378
  - On-call pager (VUH): 615-835-9798
  - On-call pager (Monroe Carell Children's): 615-835-0507
  - Automatic interpreter requests placed when LEP patients schedule appointments

TENNESSEE LANGUAGE CENTER (TLC)
  - State resource for interpretation and translation
  - Serves courts, hospitals, schools, corporations, and government agencies
  - On-site interpretation, video remote, phone interpretation available
  - Certified document translation for legal, immigration, and academic use
  - Phone: 615-741-7579
  - Email: its.languagecenter@tennessee.edu
  - Address: 193 Polk Ave., Suite B, Nashville, TN

INTERPRETER IO
  - Professional interpretation for Nashville healthcare systems and clinics
  - Languages served: Spanish, Kurdish, Arabic, Somali, Vietnamese,
    Mandarin, ASL, and more
  - On-site, video remote (VRI), and over-phone (OPI) options available
  - HIPAA-compliant platform with scheduling and audit capabilities
  - Website: interpreter.io

CREST LANGUAGE SERVICES
  - Certified document translation and interpretation in Nashville
  - Languages: Spanish, Arabic, Mandarin/Chinese, Portuguese, and more
  - Medical: patient consultations, telehealth, hospital visits
  - Legal: court hearings, depositions, immigration documents
  - Website: crestls.com

ALBORS & ALNET
  - HIPAA-compliant medical translation and interpretation
  - Works with hospitals, rehab centers, clinics, and pharmaceutical companies
  - Also serves legal, immigration, and travel/tourism sectors
  - Serves statewide throughout Tennessee
  - Website: alborsalnet.com

BRIDGES DEAF SERVICES (via VUMC)
  - ASL and deaf/hard-of-hearing support at Vanderbilt
  - Contact: 615-248-8828
  - Also accessible by email through VUMC

SECTION 4: FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE & PATIENT AID PROGRAMS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

NASHVILLE GENERAL HOSPITAL (NGH) — CHARITY CARE & FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE
  - Committed to care for all regardless of insurance or ability to pay
  - Charity program for Davidson County residents who cannot pay
  - Sliding-scale discounts for those who don't qualify for full charity care
  - Payment plans available (minimum $50/month, up to 12 months+)
  - Financial Counselor appointments: 615-341-4165
    (Mon–Fri, 8:00 AM – 4:00 PM)
  - Billing questions: 615-341-4200 | billingquestions@nashvilleha.org

NASHVILLE HEALTHCARE INCENTIVE PROGRAM
  - Zero-cost healthcare for Metro, NES, and Tennessee State employees,
    pensioners, and dependents
  - $0 copays/deductibles at Nashville General Hospital (NGH)
    and Nashville Healthcare Center (NHC)
  - No sign-up required — just show your insurance card
  - New patient appointments: 615-341-4YOU (4968)
  - Website: nashvillehealthincentive.org
  - Note: Not available if Medicare is primary insurance (federal law)

VANDERBILT UNIVERSITY MEDICAL CENTER — FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE
  - Significant discounts available based on income
  - As of April 2025: uninsured patients may receive up to 75% discount
    on eligible services at VUMC main campus
  - Wilson County Hospital: up to 80% discount
  - Bedford and Tullahoma-Harton hospitals: up to 82–84% discount
  - Contact VUMC Financial Services for application information
  - Website: vanderbilthealth.com/information/financial-assistance

NEIGHBORHOOD HEALTH
  - Safety-net provider with 10+ clinics in Nashville (12 total in Middle TN)
  - Serves patients regardless of insurance or housing status
  - Services: medical, dental, behavioral health, psychiatric care,
    prescription support, referrals, and transportation to clinics
  - Street Medicine Team: in the field 5 days/week visiting Davidson County
    encampments and communities
  - Medication management for chronic conditions (diabetes, etc.)
  - No patient turned away for inability to pay
  - Metro-funded through June 2026
  - Website: neighborhoodhealth.org

NASHVILLE GENERAL HOSPITAL FOUNDATION — SUPPORT FUNDS
  Several dedicated patient support funds:
  - Team Chad Fund: non-clinical aid for oncology patients (rent, utilities,
    food, transportation)
  - Hardy Fund: psycho-social support, Food Pharmacy, wigs, prostheses
  - Food As Medicine Fund: nutritious food for patients with chronic illness
  - Women's Health Fund: mammograms, gynecological exams, heart health
  - All Weather Wellness Fund: clothing and hygiene products
  - Health Literacy Program: help understanding medical information
  - Website: nashgenfoundation.org

TENNESSEE DEPARTMENT OF HUMAN SERVICES — FAMILIES FIRST (TANF)
  - Workforce and employment program with transportation assistance
  - For low-income families with children under 18
  - Phone: 615-313-4700
  - Address: 400 Deaderick St, Nashville, TN 37243
  - Hours: Mon–Fri, 7:30 AM – 4:30 PM

TIPS FOR ACCESSING THESE RESOURCES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  • For TennCare transportation, call at least 2 business days ahead.
  • Hospital interpreter services are FREE and legally required — always ask.
  • Financial assistance at hospitals requires an application; call early.
  • Neighborhood Health accepts walk-ins at most clinic locations.
  • The Metro Public Health Department interpreter line (615-340-8963)
    can connect you to free language help even before a clinic visit.
  • 211 Tennessee (dial 2-1-1) can connect you to many of these
    resources by phone and may be aware of additional local programs.
